Ethics Posters

As part of our ethics activities, we set up posters in common areas of the conference, where participants could express their thoughts on the ethical topics of each day. The questions were discussed with our ethics speakers and the responses were taken as starting points for the discussions following the ethics talks.

We also kept a week long poster, where we asked the participants to make a whishlist on how they would like our academic world to evolve.
